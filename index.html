I'm Kelly Tomlinson

I craft digital experiences that delight people and help solve their problems, no matter the scale. Throughout my career I’ve spent time in most areas of the product design process from discovery, feature development, architecture, user flows, visual design, product ownership, and product management. My time in all of these spaces have shaped me into an exceptional designer that advocates for the user while balancing business needs within a beautiful cross-device experience.

I’m passionate about end-to-end experiences, building and strengthening design communities, good food and good people. I believe in solving problems, sharing stories, saying thank you, and small joys.

Ian Cahill

A current work in progress, I am redesigning the website and brand direction for Ian Cahill, a Kansas City based author. The intent is to streamline his website with a hyper-focus on his recent books and excerpts. In addition, I am exploring designs for his excerpts for use on social media, online communities, and overall brand building.

Swappa Price

I had the pleasure of working with Swappa to design their first mobile app, Swappa Price - a simple way to make it easier for their customers to buy and sell their devices. The core functionality allowed users to check the pricing history using the Swappa database to check real-time value of purchasing and listing devices. The intent was a simple and easy to use app that would lay a foundation for future expansion.

Redivus Health
