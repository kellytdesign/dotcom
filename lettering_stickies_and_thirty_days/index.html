Lettering, Stickies, & Thirty Days

Handlettering has always been one of those “one day” things for me. You know what I’m talking about: one of those things that you’re both so in awe of and yet so envious of, and you tell yourself that “one day” you’ll do something about it.

For years, I sucked at doing something about it. No amount of new pens, sketchbooks, or bursts of inspiration ever translated to ink on paper.
